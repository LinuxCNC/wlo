### LinuxCNC 2.9.9 has been WITHDRAWN

Please do not update to 2.9.9 if you use probing routines. 
A fix to a long-standing bug has broken many probing routnes that were working around the bug. 
Context here
https://forum.linuxcnc.org/38-general-linuxcnc-questions/58927-g38-2-weird-behaviour

2.9.10 fixes this and can be downloaded from github:
https://github.com/LinuxCNC/linuxcnc/releases/tag/v2.9.10

Unfortunately I am having a problem with the archive signing key for Trixie onwards and so haven't been able to release to the repositories yet. 

Please bear with me while I attempt to sort this out. 

Andy Pugh, Incompetent Release Manager.
